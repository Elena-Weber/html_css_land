Our school was founded in 2020 by a well-renowned Harvard Professor Mary Smith, a Michigan native. Her progressive teaching methods, extensive use of modern technologies on all stages of the learning process and care for her students' needs drove her to the idea of creating her own educational program that has become the basis of our curriculum. We are licensed by US Department of Education and focused on your success.
